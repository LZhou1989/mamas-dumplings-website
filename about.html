Our Story

Our family came to Germany as immigrants, and like many newcomers, we opened restaurants to make a living. While our restaurants served German-adapted Chinese food to appeal to local tastes, Mama always kept our authentic Chinese heritage alive in our home kitchen.

During Chinese holidays and family celebrations, Mama would prepare traditional dishes that reminded us of our roots. Her dumplings, made with recipes passed down through generations, became the heart of our family gatherings and a way to stay connected to our culture.

Today, we're sharing Mama's authentic dumplings with you—the same recipes she made for our family celebrations, now available for your table. Every bite carries the love and tradition that kept our family connected to home.
