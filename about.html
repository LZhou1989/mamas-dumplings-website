Our Story

Our journey began when Mama moved from China to Germany, bringing with her the authentic flavors and traditional recipes passed down through generations. In our kitchen, every dumpling is handcrafted with love, using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Today, we're proud to share these authentic Chinese dumplings with our German community, offering a taste of home and tradition in every bite.
