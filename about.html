Our Story

Our story started with a journey from China to Germany, carrying a dream for a better future and the rich culinary traditions of our homeland. After years of running successful restaurants, we felt a calling to return to our roots—the authentic flavors of our childhood.

No matter the occasion, Mama always made us feel at home with her handmade dumplings, Tang Yuan, and Zhong Zi. These tastes were our connection to heritage and family, a tradition we kept alive thousands of miles away.

Mama's Dumplings is our way of sharing that warmth and authenticity with you. Our motto is to keep Chinese tradition alive and bring genuine, heartfelt food to everyone. Welcome to our family.
